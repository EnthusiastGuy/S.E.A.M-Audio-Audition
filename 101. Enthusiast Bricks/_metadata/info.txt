Enthusiast Bricks (Pack 101) is a free chiptune music pack featuring 3 original songs, each sliced into loopable and stitchable segments for flexible arrangement. It is designed to work with this audition app so you can quickly test seamless playback, transitions, and timing, and it can also be used as ready-to-build audio material for games, videos, and other creative projects. This is the first release in an ongoing series of practical audio samples.
